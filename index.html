Residency Guidelines

Welcome to the Residency Tracker comprehensive guide! This manual will walk you through everything you need to know to use the platform smoothly, whether you are a general member or an EB!

General Basics

You must use your official @dlsu.edu.ph Google account to access the tracker.

You can switch between Light and Dark mode at any time by clicking the sun/moon icon at the top right of the screen.

Right beside the theme button, you will see two mini-boxes showing exactly how many people are currently Online and Onsite.

Go into the Account menu to view your account information. Please edit the blank fields to ensure your profile is complete. This is important for record-keeping and verification of your residency hours.

Guide for Staffers

As a member, your main dashboard allows you to log your residency hours and view your personal history.

1 Clocking In
  • When you are ready to begin, click the green Clock In button.
  • Two options will appear underneath: Onsite and Online.
  • Click the one that matches your current setup. Your timer will start immediately, and a live duration counter will appear on screen.
2 Clocking Out
  • When you are done, click the red Clock Out button.
  • Your session ends, your total accumulated hours update automatically, and your live timer disappears.
3 Tracking Your Activity
  • The main panel shows your last 5 logs, indicating whether you clocked in or out, and whether it was an online or onsite session.
  • By opening the menu (top right) and selecting Log History, you can view a complete, expanded list of all your past clock-ins and outs with exact dates and timestamps.

Guide for EBs

You will see an Admin Board option in your menu. This gives you extra tools to manage the roster and members.

1 Active Sessions

This section displays a live list of everyone currently clocked in.

You can see their email and exactly what time they started their shift (including whether they are working online or onsite).

2 Roster Management
  • The Roster displays all members in the system, their total verified hours, and a status dot (green for clocked-in, grey for clocked-out).
  • You can sort the list to show "Clocked In First" or "Clocked Out First" using the dropdown menu.
  • Force In / Force Out: If a member forgets to clock in or out, you can manually toggle their status.
  • Purge / Delete: You can completely wipe a user's log history and hours if necessary. Please avoid clicking accidentally. THIS WILL DELETE THEIR HISTORY PERMANENTLY.
3 Assigning New Admins

At the bottom of the Admin Board, there is a field to "Give Admin Privileges".

Type in the user's @dlsu.edu.ph email address and click Add. They will instantly gain administrative access.

Reminders

Don't worry about leaving a session open overnight. The system automatically performs a midnight reset and clocks out any remaining active sessions so that hours are recorded properly.

Need Help? If your total hours or occupancy numbers do not immediately update, simply refresh the page to pull the freshest data from the server. If you think there is a mistake, contact an EB.
