Website Guidelines

Welcome to the PropMan Lobby!

This guide covers everything — from tracking residency hours to requesting folios. Use the quick-jump below or scroll through.

Getting Started

  • Sign in with your official @dlsu.edu.ph Google account.
  • Toggle between Light and Dark mode using the sun/moon icon in the header.
  • The Online and Onsite mini-boxes show how many people are currently active.
  • Open the Account Information menu to edit your profile — Position, Section, Full Name, Nickname, Pronouns, and ID Number.
  • Use the Send Feedback / Report Bug button (bug icon) to submit ratings, feedback, or bug reports.

Residency Tracker

Clocking In
  • Click the green Clock In button, then choose Onsite or Online.
  • A live duration counter will appear showing how long you've been clocked in.
Clock-in is restricted on Sundays and outside working hours (10 PM – 7 AM). You won't be able to clock in during those times. If you're an EB and absolutely need to, you can override with a confirmation prompt.
Clocking Out
  • When you are done, click the red Clock Out button.
  • Your session ends, total hours update, and the timer disappears.
Important: Always remember to clock out when you leave the office! If you forget, the system will auto-clock you out at the end of the day, but it's better to do it yourself so your hours are recorded accurately.
Tracking Your Activity
  • Your main panel shows your last 5 logs — clock-ins and outs with mode (Online/Onsite).
  • Select Log History from the menu to see your complete record with dates and timestamps.

Get a Folio

Browsing
  • Each folio card shows an image, the folio name, the total stock, and the per-member limit.
  • Use the search bar to filter folios by name.
  • Click on a folio image to open a full-size preview.
Ordering
  • Use the + and − buttons to set the quantity for each folio. Each folio has a per-order maximum enforced by the input's limit.
  • Your cart shows a summary of all selected items with quantities.
  • Click Get Selected to submit your request.
Quantity Limits & EB Password
  • Each folio type has a per-member limit set by the EBs. If a folio has a limit of 3, you can only take up to 3 of that folio type total.
  • If you want more than the per-member limit, you'll need an EB password to proceed.
  • Once verified, the approval lasts for your entire session — you won't need to re-enter it.
Folios marked DO NOT DISTRIBUTE are not available for distribution. Contact an EB if you think this is a mistake.

EB Password System

  • EBs can set a personal password from the Admin Board. It can be anything, make it fun!
  • This password lets members approve folio requests over the per-member limit. If you're a member, ask an EB for their password.
  • Passwords reset every Saturday at 10 PM for security. EBs will need to set a new one after reset.
  • If an EB hasn't set a password yet, they'll be prompted to create one when they log in.

Feedback & Bug Reports

  • Click the bug icon in the header to open the feedback modal.
  • Switch between Feedback (with star ratings) or Bug Report mode.
  • Rate your experience and how you found the website.
  • Write a message and hit Submit — we read every submission!

Guide for EBs

The Admin Board option in the menu gives you extra tools to manage the roster and members.

Active Sessions

Displays a live list of everyone currently clocked in, their email, and start time (with Online/Onsite mode).

Roster Management
  • Shows all members, their total verified hours, a status dot (green = clocked in, grey = clocked out), and their section.
  • Sort the list with the dropdown: Clocked In First or Clocked Out First. You can also filter by section.
  • Click on a member to view their full account information.
  • Force In / Force Out: Manually toggle a member's status if they forgot to clock in or out.
  • Purge: Completely wipe a user's log history and hours. This is permanent — use with care!
Log Missed Hours

If a member forgot to clock in, use the Log Missed Hours section to add hours directly. Enter the member's email and the number of hours (positive to add, negative to deduct). This logs an entry in the Res_Log sheet.

Assigning New Admins

Type a @dlsu.edu.ph email in the "Give Admin Privileges" field and click Add. They'll gain administrative access instantly.

Set Your EB Password
  • In the Admin Board, you'll find a section to set your EB Password.
  • This password is used when members need to request more than the per-folio limit. Share it with them when they ask!
  • Passwords reset every Saturday at 10 PM, so you'll need to set a new one each week.
  • If you haven't set one yet, you'll see a prompt when you log in.

Reminders

  • Always clock out when you leave the office! The system does auto-clock-out at midnight as a safety net, but manual clock-out ensures your hours are most accurate.
  • If your total hours or occupancy numbers don't update immediately, refresh the page to pull the latest data.
  • Think something is wrong? Contact an EB or submit a Bug Report from the header.
